OUR MODEL

Every girl educated

With common barriers like poverty, food insecurity, healthcare access, and psychosocial well-being mitigated, WISER unlocks girls’ potential through exceptional academic instruction by local educators, enrichments in STEM, the arts, and athletics, and peer-to-peer and mentor led psychosocial support.

Every girl supported

WISER provides each and every one of its students with everything they need to succeed at school. And that means everything: from school supplies and menstrual hygiene products, to uniforms and three meals a day, all under a safe roof with caring adult mentors and educators.

Every girl healthy

WISER’s “whole girl” approach to wellness recognizes that mental, physical, emotional, and sexual well-being are integrally linked and foundational to both individual and community success. That means WISER girls are not only healthy, they are champions of their own community’s well-being.

OUR OUTCOMES (SINCE INCEPTION)

We believe in a future of women, imagining and building technology that will mirror the people and societies for which they build. We seek to be a part of the solution on a continent where youth constitute 37% of the working-age population but, tragically, 60% of the unemployed. We provide practical, hands-on skills training that prepares young women for existing opportunities in the labor market.
